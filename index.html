Neon Shader

Shader presentation that showcases neon like color amplification and smooth color transition over time. Transition duration, intensity and color amplification intensity can be adjusted via sliders. Shader effect can be turned on and off.

Sugarscape

Sugarscape is a model that simulates social behaviours. In this version we have 3 tribes of ants that gather resources to survive. Each turn ants eat sugar and spice from their backpack, gather resources from the place they are and move elsewhere.

Tanks

"Tanks" is a simple game where you try to destroy other tanks. Use W,S to move forward and backwards, A,D to rotate towards left and right, and Space to shoot.
